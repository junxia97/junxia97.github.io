Profile

Jun Xia is a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), leading the AI for Multimodality and Science (AIMS) Lab with research focused on multimodal large models and AI for life sciences. He also holds an affiliated appointment at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Clear Water Bay, Hong Kong). He received his Ph.D. from a joint program between Westlake University and Zhejiang University.
