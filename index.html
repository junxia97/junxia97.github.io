Profile

Hi there! I am Jun Xia, a Ph.D. student at Westlake University and Zhejiang University, advised by Chair Prof. Stan Z. Li. Before Joining Westlake, I received my B.E. degree with honors from Central South University. My primary research interests lie in the intersection of Machine Learning and applications in Computational Omics.
